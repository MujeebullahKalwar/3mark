Sales & Marketing – Scott Meyers


Sales and Marketing Team:

Scott Meyers

Sales Director

As Sales Director, Scott Meyers brings more than 14 years experience to 3Marks insurance producers and financial planners. Using his vast knowledge of advanced case design, sales strategies, estate and wealth preservation, Scott provides outstanding support and guidance assisting producers with cases and directing them to the right fit from our broad array of top rated carriers and competitive products. Prior to joining 3Mark in 2014, Scott held various sales positions at national firms.

Scott is a graduate of the University of Houston, where he received a Bachelors in Business Marketing. He is securities licensed Series 6 and 63; and a member of The National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors (NAIFA) Texas.
